# AI Chatbot Platform

A production-ready, enterprise-grade AI chatbot platform that automatically transforms into a personalized business assistant by analyzing any website. Simply provide a website URL, and the platform intelligently crawls pages, extracts business information, understands brand identity, and builds a fully customized AI chatbot—without manual training, coding, or prompt engineering.

Designed with scalability, security, and maintainability in mind, the platform follows modern software engineering practices using Next.js, React, TypeScript, Tailwind CSS, shadcn/ui, Node.js, PostgreSQL, Prisma, and AI models from OpenAI or Anthropic.

## Key Features

* 🤖 Automatic website analysis and AI training
* 🌐 Intelligent business knowledge extraction
* 💬 Personalized chatbot for any industry
* 🎨 Brand-aware responses and communication style
* 📚 FAQ, services, products, and content understanding
* ⚡ Enterprise-grade architecture and modular design
* 🔒 Secure, scalable, and production-ready foundation
* 📈 Built for future integrations including CRM, WhatsApp, analytics, voice, multilingual support, and more

The goal is to provide businesses with a plug-and-play AI assistant that can be deployed in minutes while maintaining the flexibility and architecture required for long-term growth.
